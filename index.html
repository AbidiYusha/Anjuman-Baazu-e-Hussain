Who We Are

A Legacy of Devotion & Service

Anjuman Baazu-e-Hussain is a registered religious and social organization dedicated to commemorating the martyrdom of Hazrat Imam Hussain (A.S.) and the Ahle Bait (A.S.) through Azadari, Matam, and community service.

Founded on the principles of Ahal-e-Bait with love, devotion, and sacrifice, our Anjuman serves the humanity under the teachings of our beloved Imam Ali Ibn Abi Talib (A.S.) and our beloved Prophet Muhammad (S.A.W.W.S.).

"Hussain (A.S.) is the lamp of guidance and the ark of salvation."

We organize Majalis, Juloos, Matam, Sabeel, and various welfare activities throughout the year — especially during the sacred months of Muharram and Safar.

Our Activities

Majalis-e-Aza
Organizing Majalis throughout the year to commemorate the martyrdom of Imam Hussain (A.S.) and Ahle Bait (A.S.)
🚶
Matami Juloos
Participating in and organizing mourning processions on Ashura, Chehlum and other sacred occasions
🎙️
Nauha & Marsiya
Conducting Nauha, Marsiya and religious programs to keep alive the message of Karbala
💧
Sabeel & Langar
Organizing Sabeel (water distribution) and Langar to serve the community during Muharram
🤝
Community Service
Serving the needy, helping families in distress and contributing to the welfare of the community
